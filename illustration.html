Inktober 2018

Inktober is a yearly event created by artist Jake Parker to practice and draw in ink. Every October, artists worldwide participate, following the official prompt list or coming up with their own.

For 2018 I drew my take on the prompt list, by interpreting it through a series of windows. The following images were done digitally in Procreate.

Inktober 2016

For Inktober 2016, I drew images based on songs that I was listening to at the time. This year I used traditional tools: copics, brush pens, markers, fountain pens, and fineliners.
