The Project

Amorphous is a 3D animated short about an astronaut on an alien planet and his encounter with a strange creature. This short was created over a period of 3 months for an introductory animation course.

I worked on the narrative concept, modelling and rigging of the main character and a portion of the art assets, and editing together a section of the final film.
